NEW YEAR CHALLENGE | 500KMs IN JANUARY

BBCh New Year Challenge is the perfect opportunity to start the year with 500+ kms in the month of January.

500KMs in the month of JANUARY | It’s challenge, not a Race

500 km Finisher will get a Medal / Momento and goodies at the end of February 2021 to your provided address.

- Ride as per your convenient time and route. Follow government guidelines
- Challenge Date: 1st January 2021 to 31st January 2021
- 500km in the month of January 2021. You can start riding on any date between 1st January 2021 and 31st January 2021. Consecutive day rides are not necessary. Total 500km is required in January month.
- Only 500 km will be counted: There is no extra credit for more distance rides and there are no leaderboard ranks for this challenge.
- There is no time limit for the rides. There is no extra credit for completing 500km faster and there is no leaderboard ranking for this challenge.
- No indoor or on trainer rides will count.
- Above 7+ age can participate in the challenge.
- All bicycles will be allowed. (electric bicycle not allowed)
- We will consider Strava, Garmin or any GPS tracking device considered. Kindly log or port your data to Strava, Garmin for the Challenge.
- Each individual is responsible for his own health & safety and Bangalore - Bicycle Championships (BBCh) is not responsible for any casualty. By entering into the challenge you accept the above.
- BBCh decision is final.
- Kindly follow all Covid19 regulations and guidelines during the ride.

*** Registration is non-refundable and non-transferable under any circumstances.

**** This is a self-supported Sports challenge and you are responsible to take care of your safety and health

For any further information, contact us at council@bbch.in or +91 9886046777

Note: Registration will be closed by Saturday, 23rd January (midnight)

What do I need to do after completion of 500km in January 2021?

- STRAVA USERS: Once you done the registration, you will get one link to Join the club - New Year Challenge | 500km in January. Its mandatory to join this club.
- If not SRAVA: All other apps users such as Garmin, Endomondo, Apple app, Map My Ride, Tomtom, Samsung Health, MI fit band, Amazefit watch, etc need to submit their data manually from 1st February 2021 to 5th February 2021.
- Share your January month’s activity on 1st February 2021 to 5th February 2021 to council@bbch.in OR Whatsapp Number +91 9886046777
- Data submission dates will remain active for up to 5 days from 1st February 2021. After that, we will not consider your data.
- We will start shipping your finisher goodies from 15th February onwards. Due to the pandemic situation delivery services are taking time so please be patient. The finisher goodies will be delivered to you within 20 - 25 Days.
